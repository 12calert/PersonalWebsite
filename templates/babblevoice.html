What is Babblevoice?

Babblevoice is a smart cloud-hosted telephony system built specifically for GP surgeries. It is simple to use, yet meets the complex needs of todayâ€™s GP practices, CCGs and PCNs. We excel in areas such as cost effectiveness, line capacity, flexibility, remote-working, and real-time reporting.

Our mission is to deliver a phone system that supports surgeries in delivering care to patients, with functionality that includes:

  • intelligent call routing and queuing enabling telephone triage
  • integration with EMIS and SystmOne allowing secure automated access to patient records
  • automated patient-led booking and managing of appointments
  • call recording for monitoring and training
  • real-time reporting
