Viridiastrum Vexillum

This game has been developed by me over the course of my studies and served as a showcase of my design and execution of large software projects.

The project features:

  • A 2D game engine developed on top of pygame
  • Custom map builder allowing for quick creation of TMX maps
  • Dynamic quest system
  • Ability, quest and dialogue creator
  • Complex progression system of characters with different classes
  • Non-linear development of player characters through use of talent trees
