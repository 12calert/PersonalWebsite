Experiment.io

This project aims to develop a web-based platform for clients to create experiments and gather data automacally. The website features the MapTask experiment which allows 2 players to co-operate to navigate through a map of obstacles. There are plans for further experiment development.

Features

  • mutliplayer environment with different roles
  • custom experiments and conditions ( eg. disabled chat or other gameplay mechanics )
  • data visualisation
  • researcher login
  • real-time reporting

Importance of Teamwork

Teamwork was at the heart of our endeavor as we collaboratively embarked on developing an innovative web app catered to the unique needs of psychology researcher at our university. Together, we formed a diverse blend of skills, perspectives, and passions, seamlessly working towards a shared goal. Our combined efforts merged web development with psychological experiments, creating an environment where creativity thrived. Through open communication, mutual respect, and a common vision, we empowered each team member to contribute their expertise and ideas. As we moved forward, embracing challenges as opportunities and celebrating milestones, our commitment to teamwork became the foundation of a successfull project.
